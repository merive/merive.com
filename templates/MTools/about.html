About MTools

MTools is free open-source software for your tasks.
There you can find simple applications: Note, Calculator, Email, Time and RRC.
Use Note for quick editing of text files, Calculator for simple counting, Email for email sending, Time for viewing time and RRC for message encrypting/decrypting.
All this applications in one bundle - MTools.
